# Florida East Coast Data Sources - Resource Sheet

## Indian River County

### Foreclosure Records
- **Source**: Indian River County Clerk of Circuit Court
- **URL**: https://indianriverclerk.com/foreclosure-cases/
- **Online Case Search**: https://indianriverclerk.com/court-records/online-case-view/
- **Access**: Free public access, no registration required
- **Contact**: Civil Department - (772) 226-3100
- **Address**: 2000 16th Avenue, Room 158, Vero Beach, FL 32960
- **Data Available**: Case numbers, filing dates, plaintiff/defendant, amounts, hearing dates

### Tax Delinquency Records
- **Source**: Indian River County Tax Collector
- **Tax Deed Info**: https://indianriverclerk.com/recording-services/tax-deeds/
- **Delinquent Tax Dept**: (772) 226-3100
- **Tax Search**: https://services.irctax.com/
- **Data Available**: Delinquent taxes, tax certificate holders, tax sale dates

### Property Records
- **Source**: Indian River County Property Appraiser
- **URL**: https://www.ircpa.org/
- **Property Search**: https://qpublic.schneidercorp.com/Application.aspx?App=IndianRiverCountyFL&PageType=Search
- **Access**: Free public access
- **Search By**: Owner name, address, parcel number, legal description
- **Data Available**: Property values, ownership, sales history

### Foreclosure Auctions
- **URL**: https://indian-river.realforeclose.com/
- **Access**: Public auction listings
