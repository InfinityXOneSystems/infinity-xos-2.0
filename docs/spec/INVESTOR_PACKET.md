## 2. The Problem
**The Human Bottleneck:**
Global GDP is capped by human cognitive limits. Businesses today face three critical friction points:
1.  **Latency:** Human analysis and decision-making are too slow for real-time markets.
2.  **Scalability:** Hiring, training, and managing human teams is linear and expensive.
3.  **Fragmented Data:** Business intelligence is siloed across dozens of tools, making holistic automated decision-making impossible.

**Market Gap:**
Current AI tools (ChatGPT, Midjourney) are "assistants"—they wait for prompts. There is no enterprise-grade ecosystem for *autonomous* agents that proactively identify problems and execute solutions without human intervention.
